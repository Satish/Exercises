1. Write a query to select post_ids that have both 1 and 3 as tag_ids

SELECT t1.*, t2.tag_id FROM tags t1 INNER JOIN tags t2 ON t1.post_id=t2.post_id WHERE t1.tag_id=1 AND t2.tag_id=3;
